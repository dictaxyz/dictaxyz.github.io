Frequently asked questions.

When does an attorney-client relationship begin?

Nothing on this website should be considered legal advice. Nor should anything on this website be construed as triggering any attorney-client relationship. No attorney-client relationship begins until you have been sent an Engagement Letter through the portal, signed and returned it to us.

Does the free compliance document pack constitute legal advice?

The documents are merely self-help services provided at your specific direction. The documents are not a substitute for legal advice.

Are consultations free?

Users are welcome to correspond and send documents through the portal. While there is no free live consultation-- we do consult through the portal for free. If you prefer a live consultation, schedule one using our Calendar .

What is the fee for non-flat fee services?

The hourly billable rate for non-packaged services is $500/hr.
